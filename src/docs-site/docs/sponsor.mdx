## 1. Why Sponsorship Matters

<div class="csnCard">

Building an AI-powered security platform requires significant resources, including:

- AI model training infrastructure
- Dataset collection and verification
- Cybersecurity research
- Backend and extension development
- Continuous monitoring and maintenance

Sponsors help us **accelerate innovation** and **expand protection for families and schools**.
